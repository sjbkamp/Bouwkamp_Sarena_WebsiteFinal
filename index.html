Mission

Kasama is a modern American resturant with a flavorful Mediteranian twist. The dishes here are bright and colorful and good for the soul. Our goal here at Kasama is to make delicious food and keep a friendly welcomeing enviroment to all people. Our mission is to provide a modern friendly setting to accomodate you and all your needs. We hope to be a friendly smile and create dishes that will make you smile even more. Every day we strive to adapt and learn more to keep our establisment informed and working towards meeting every need of our customer. We look forward to seeing you!

Owners

The owners of this restuant are Genie Kwon and Timothy Flores. They both are located in the East Ukrainian Village neighborhood of Chicago. Genie and Timothy both grew up in Morroco wich inspired them to bring a little taste of home to America. They have a passion for their food and wanted to combine their childhood favorite to already established American dishes. They met after their move to America and it was then that they discovered their mutal passion for Mediteranian food. They got to work and after many years in the works established the resturant in 1997. They both are very involved in hellping the cooks to prepare the dishes ensuring that each one brings a flavorful unique taste to you!
